About Student.INFO

AWARDED AS BEST HACK FOR STUDENTS @ 16-17.05.2015, DRAGONHACK, LJUBLJANA, SI

Student.INFO is a webpage that gathers all relevant educational info from E-classroom, STUDIS exam data system, timetables and relevant Facebook feed on a single page.
Student.INFO is designed and developed by four students (Team Frikoti) for other students at University of Ljubljana, Faculty of computer and information science
